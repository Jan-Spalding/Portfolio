20th June 2023

Sniping Montage:

This was my beginning and started with a few clips from a friend. Video contains a few simple cuts as well as a hand tracked mask to act as a transition. From here my friend would every so often supply me with his highlights and I used them as an excuse to practice my editing.

2nd September 2023

COD Montage:

By this point I had put in a lot of time trying to better understand the program that I was using. I had experimented with adjustment layers which allowed me to add effects as well as some basic zooms. With the addition of sound effects ontop of the music as game audio was muted. Even experimented with slowing down the clips at certain points but hadn't quite got the timing right. First evidence of me trying to sync up the kills with the beats of the song too.
